# Roadmap

The Stay Loyal roadmap is intentionally focused on building strong foundations before expanding into larger ecosystem features.

Our goal is not to build the most features.

Our goal is to build the right features in the right order.

---

# Phase 1 — Loyalty Foundation

Build the core systems required to establish loyalty as a measurable signal.

### Completed

- Wallet Connection
- NFT Discovery
- NFT Eligibility Verification
- NFT Staking
- Loyalty XP Engine
- Rank Progression System
- Loyalty Dashboard
- Lifetime Loyalty Tracking
- Lock Period Support
- Community Participation Metrics

### Outcome

A holder can connect a wallet, stake eligible NFTs, earn loyalty XP, progress through ranks, and begin building a measurable loyalty profile.

Status: Completed

---

# Phase 2 — Founder Infrastructure

Provide NFT communities with the tools needed to understand and manage loyalty within their ecosystem.

### In Progress

- Founder Dashboard
- Collection Analytics
- Holder Analytics
- Loyalty Leaderboards
- NFT Tracking
- Community Participation Metrics
- Reward Configuration
- Community Settings
- Access Enforcement

### Outcome

Community founders gain visibility into their most loyal members and can begin building loyalty-driven experiences.

Status: In Progress

---

# Phase 3 — Community Experience

Transform loyalty from a hidden metric into a visible and engaging community experience.

### Planned

- NFT Evolution System
- Evolution Eligibility Tracking
- Evolution History
- Loyalty Achievements
- Community Milestones
- Progression Visualization
- Loyalty Badges

### Outcome

Members can see their progress, celebrate milestones, and build a stronger emotional connection with the community.

Status: Planned

---

# Phase 4 — Community Integrations

Connect existing community platforms into the Stay Loyal ecosystem.

### Planned

- Public Community URLs
- Discord OAuth
- Twitter/X OAuth
- Telegram OAuth
- Community Verification Systems

### Outcome

Communities can integrate loyalty directly into the platforms where their members already participate.

Status: Planned

---

# Phase 5 — Engagement Infrastructure

Introduce systems that encourage meaningful participation beyond NFT ownership.

### Planned

- Community Missions
- Community Challenges
- Activity-Based Loyalty
- Contribution Tracking
- Participation Rewards

### Outcome

Loyalty becomes a combination of ownership, participation, and contribution.

Status: Planned

---

# Phase 6 — Reputation Layer

Expand loyalty beyond individual communities.

### Future Vision

- Multi-Community Membership
- Cross-Community Reputation
- Wallet Reputation Profiles
- Global Loyalty Score
- Community Reputation Graph

### Outcome

A wallet's history becomes larger than any individual collection.

Members carry their reputation across multiple ecosystems while communities maintain independent loyalty systems.

Status: Future Vision

---

# Phase 7 — Ecosystem Intelligence

Provide founders with deeper insights into community health and engagement.

### Future Vision

- Community Discovery
- Referral Infrastructure
- Growth Analytics
- Loyalty Insights
- AI-Powered Community Intelligence

### Outcome

Founders gain actionable insights into engagement, retention, and long-term community growth.

Status: Future Vision

---

# What We Are Not Building Yet

Stay Loyal intentionally avoids certain features until the core loyalty experience has been validated.

### Not Yet

- DAO Voting
- Token Rewards
- Public Community Discovery
- Multi-Community Membership
- AI Features
- Referral Systems

These features may become valuable in the future, but strong foundations come first.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Stay Loyal is evolving toward a future where loyalty becomes a measurable, portable, and valuable asset within Web3 communities.

The destination is not simply NFT staking.

The destination is loyalty infrastructure for the entire NFT ecosystem.
